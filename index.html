About Me

Full stack developer with a biomedical and software engineering background. Recently received UT Austins Full Stack Bootcamp certification with past certifications in Python and JavaScript. I also have experience in building mobile applications (XCode, Unity) as well as hardware devices (Arduino, Raspberry Pi). My passion in software is to develop beautiful web and mobile applications that positively change our world. I am excited to utilize my Full Stack toolset as well as my past software engineering skills to leave a lasting impact on this world through the beauty of software.
